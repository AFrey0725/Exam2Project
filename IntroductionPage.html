Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is a computer science field that involves creating systems that can mimic human intelligence and utilize problem- solving skills.

AI can learn from data, adjust to new inputs, and perform tasks that are usually performed by humans.

Artificial intelligence is a broad field that includes several different technologies. Some of these technologies inclue: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Examples of Artificial Intelligence:

  • Chess-playing computers
  • Self-driving cars
  • Chat GPT
  • Computer vision
