Artificial Intelligence Advancements

The field of AI is constantly evolving with new techniques, algorithms, and methodologies being developed.

AI can analyze vast amounts of data in real time to improve effieciency, and accuracy in data-drven-decision-making processes.

In the last 10 years, AI systems have developed at a rapid speed. AI is now able to recognize images and speech better than humans. AI can also pass tests including business school exams and Amazon coding interview questions.

Potential of AI

AI has the potential to fuel economic growth and make the world better in many ways, including:

improving decison-making
improving access to services
improving environmental sustainability
improving productivity
preserving historical documents
improving medical care/diagnosis
