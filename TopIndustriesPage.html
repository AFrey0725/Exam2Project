Top Industries Using AI

The financial services industry is the global leader in using AI, with banking and insurance being the most advanced users. AI is used in financial services for a variety of purposes, including: Fraud detection and prevention: AI is used to increase the accuracy of financial forecasting and fraud detection. Credit risk management: AI is used to assess borrowers' creditworthiness, and risk Customer support and personal banking.

Other Industries that are using AI include:

Healthcare: AI is used to improve treatment efficacy and speed up diagnosis. Industrial and energy: AI is used to increase efficiency, reduce manufacturing stoppages, and automate processes. Education: AI is used to improve the learning experience. Security and surveillance: AI is used to anticipate crime. Human resources: AI is used to optimize recruitment and talent management. Oil and gas: AI is used to access data for oil and gas exploration. Cloud pricing optimization: AI is used to reduce cloud costs and find cost-effective solutions for running cloud applications.
